Who competes in WSL CT?:
The season starts with 36 Surfers:
32 qualified surfers, 2 season wildcards, and 2 event wildcards

Mid-Season cut-off: The field is reduced to the top 22 surfers + 2 event wildcards for each event

Finals: Finals consist of the top 5 surfers ranked by total points, and they compete in a 1 day tournment where the winner is crowned the Champion

*** 6 of 9 United States of America Surfers are from Hawaii resulting in USA not being as dominent as they appear

How does the points system work?
Each event is styled as a bracketed heats with the last rounds consisting of head to head match-ups. In heat's Surfers are compete based off the combined score of their top 2 waves which are judged from 0-10

The Final 5:
After 11 events the top 5 surfers based off total points face off for the world title
Match 1 Rank #5 vs Rank #4 (Elimination)
Match 2 Winner of Match 1 vs Rank #3 (Elimination)
Match 3 Winner of Match 2 vs Rank #2 (Elimination)
Title Match Winner of Match 3 vs Rank #1 (Rank #1 win first Heat or Best of 3 heats)
